How to use ContentDrop 👑
3 simple steps. even if you've never used AI before, you've got this.
1
Add your content to the board
See the buttons at the top? Click + Note to type an idea. Click + YouTube to paste a YouTube link. Click + PDF to upload a document. Click + Reference to add your brand info. Click + TikTok / IG / FB to analyze videos from those platforms.

Each one creates a card on your board. Think of cards like sticky notes 📝
2
Click a card to select it
Click any card on the board. It will glow red — that means it's selected ✅

Want to use multiple cards together? Hold Shift and click more cards. ContentDrop will read ALL of them when creating your content.

💡 Tip: select your brand card + a YouTube card together to get content that matches your voice!
3
Pick what you want to create
Look at the right side panel — it shows everything ContentDrop can make for you. Things like:

📸 IG Caption — a ready-to-post Instagram caption
🎬 TikTok Script — a full script to record
📧 Full Email — a marketing email
📖 Ebook — a complete ebook (you pick how many pages!)

Just click any of them and your content appears in seconds ⚡
4
Copy it and post it!
When your content appears at the bottom of the screen, click Copy to copy it. Then paste it wherever you need it — Instagram, TikTok, your email tool, anywhere.

Hit Save to keep it in your Library so you can come back to it anytime.
💡 The golden rule: Add cards first → Select cards → Click what you want to make. That's it! ContentDrop does the rest. You don't need to know anything about AI to use this. If it creates something you don't love, just click again — you'll get a different result every time!
